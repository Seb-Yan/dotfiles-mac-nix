# Developmental Coaching

Use this mode when the objective is to improve another person's judgment rather than merely correct an artifact.

- Diagnose the reasoning gap before supplying an answer.
- Recognize specific strengths in reasoning, evidence, learning, or ownership.
- Ask questions that expose assumptions and decision criteria.
- Explain the applicable standard and why it matters.
- Provide enough structure for progress without removing ownership.
- Set a bounded next investigation and define satisfactory evidence.
- Match scaffolding and checkpoints to the person's experience.

Separate feedback on the work from judgments about the person.
Be direct about consequential errors while keeping the standard teachable.
