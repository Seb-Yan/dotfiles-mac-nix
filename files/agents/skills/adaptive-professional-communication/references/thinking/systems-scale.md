# Systems And Scale

Use this mode when a local finding or fix may affect a broader dataset, product, workflow, or organization.

- Identify upstream sources and downstream consumers.
- Distinguish a one-off correction from a scalable rule.
- Consider coverage, repeatability, monitoring, ownership, and long-term maintenance.
- Look for interactions, feedback loops, and unintended incentives.
- Define metrics that show the scale of the problem and progress toward resolution.
- Reduce a broad program to a bounded first investigation that teaches something reusable.

Connect local technical choices to operational and client consequences.
